Lectures on Higher Categories

March 2025

Plan

  • Part 1: (∞,1)-categories: models and practice, universal constructions, (un)straightening equivalence, cofinality, Yoneda and presheaves, Presentable categories and localizations, stable categories, spectra, associative and commutative algebras, animation, monadicity.
  • Special talk on enriched ∞-categories by Markus Zetto.
  • Part 2: the (∞,1)-category of (∞,n)-categories. Strict methods and the Steiner theory. Lax transformations and Gray tensor product. Categorical Spectra and stability. Adjunction, TQFTs and factorization homology.
